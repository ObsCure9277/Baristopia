Our History

Our story begin in a richful taste country, Malaysia, 1970.
In 1970, our founder, Raden Tan Sheng Hao and Dylan Low Sheng wei had started to sell coffee in a coffee shop (or more authentic name, "kopitiam") in Taman Bunga Raya, Setapak, Kuala Lumpur. During that time, the coffee shop's name was "Sheng Coffee". As our founders sold good quality and tasteful coffee, total sales of coffee had increased rapidly and soon became famous in the area nearby.

When I was small, I started to drink the coffee at Sheng Coffee. It was my flavor breakfast drink. Their coffee had a strong flavor and after I drank it, I could work very well. The taste is a testament to the dedication of the baristas who have honed their craft over time, to the beans that have been carefully selected from far-flung corners of the world, and to the generations of patrons who have found solace in these hallowed halls.

---Mr Kong Jian Hong (resident of Taman Bunga Raya)





As Sheng Coffee had became more popular, they decided to change the coffee shop to Pasar Seni. In order to attract more customers to enjoy the coffee, Dylan Low went to Italy to learn to brew Italic style coffee, then brought back the technics to Malaysia.


After studied the way to brew Italic style coffee, Dylan Low invented our most significant coffee, which is Asian Dolce Latte. To integrate the Italic environment, our founder had changed the name from Sheng Coffee to Baristopia and renovated the coffee shop.


After our founder invented Asian Dolce Latte, Baristopia had won many awards including Best Invention of Coffee of the Year, Baristo'Compete, Top Selling Coffee in Malaysia, International Coffee Festival, Michellin Star Awards and so on.


---Baristopia's significant coffee, Asian Dolce Latte---
